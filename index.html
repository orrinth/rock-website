Types of Rocks

Rocks come in all shapes and sizes. For instance, one can pick up a pebble, but can't lift a canyon. Shapes and sizes of rocks aren't the only properties to differ, however. In fact, the ways that rocks are formed also vary, which are grouped into three main types. These are Igneous, Sedimentary, and Metamorphic.
